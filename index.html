How It Works

Simple setup, powerful protection

1

Install & Setup

Download the app to your smartwatch and complete a quick setup process. Add your emergency contacts and customize your preferences.

2

Continuous Monitoring

The app runs in the background, continuously measuring your heart rate, blood pressure, and blood oxygen levels throughout the day.

3

Intelligent Analysis

Our medical-grade algorithms analyze your vitals in real-time, learning your baseline and detecting any concerning deviations.

4

Automatic Alerts

If a fall is detected or vitals fall outside safe ranges, the app immediately sends notifications to your emergency contacts with your location and status.

5

Manual Updates

You can also manually update your status at any time using a button or voice command to let your contacts know you're fine or need assistance.
